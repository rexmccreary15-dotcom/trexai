# Disable Email Confirmation in Supabase

To completely remove the email confirmation requirement:

1. **Go to Supabase dashboard**: https://supabase.com/dashboard
2. **Select your project**
3. **Click "Authentication"** â†’ **"Providers"** (left sidebar)
4. **Find "Email"** provider
5. **Scroll down** to find **"Confirm email"** setting
6. **Toggle "Confirm email" to OFF** (disable it)
7. **Click "Save"**

After this, users can sign up and immediately log in without needing to verify their email.
